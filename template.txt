4. At Least Three Example Tasks
Prompt: Give three concrete examples of what a user might try to do with your system.

Example Task 1 (for category: Health information seeking / decision support)
A patient who was recently diagnosed with Type 2 diabetes wants help understanding the diagnosis, comparing treatment options, and identifying lifestyle changes by interacting with a chatbot grounded in uploaded materials such as clinic handouts, provider-approved educational documents, and medication guides.

Example Task 2 (for category: Conceptual understanding / learning)
An undergraduate student studying for an earth science exam wants help understanding how lightning forms, including charge separation in clouds, the role of ice particles and collisions, and how a stepped leader and return stroke produce the visible lightning flash, using uploaded course notes, textbook excerpts, and diagrams.

Example Task 3 (for category: Creative information seeking)
A hobbyist game designer wants help developing a cozy mystery video game concept set in a small coastal town, including brainstorming characters, settings, mechanics, and narrative arcs, using uploaded inspiration documents such as story notes, art references, design guidelines, and examples of similar games.
